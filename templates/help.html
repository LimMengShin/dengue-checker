How to use this dengue checker

For each of the 14 questions, answer "Yes" or "No" by clicking on the radio button.

After answering all the questions, click on the "Submit" button to submit your answers.

If not all the questions are answered when the "Submit" button is clicked, the answers will not be submitted. Instead, the website will prompt an alert to inform you to answer all the questions. Please ensure that all the questions are answered before submitting again.

If there is no internet connection when the "Submit" button is clicked, the website will prompt an alert, asking you if you still want to submit.
If you click on the "OK" button, the answers will be submitted. However, the dengue checker will be unable to check for dengue clusters near you.
If you click on the "Cancel" button, the answers will not be submitted and the alert will disappear.

After submitting, you will be redirected to the "Results" page.

This "Results" page will display the chance of you having dengue and advice on what to do. It will also display the dengue clusters nearby, if any.

How this dengue checker works

First, it calculates the percentage from your answers you input.
Each question has a different weightage and the it is divided by the total questions to get a percentage.

Then we get your longditude and latitude of your device and check it with the longditute and latitude of dengue clusters from the NEA website.
The weightage of this would depend on how many clusters your device is near to and add to the percentage

However, if your percentage after adding the value is above 100% it will not be added in.
